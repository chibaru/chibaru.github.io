About

My goal after graduating from university with a degree in journalism many years ago was not thought out too well because I just wanted to get done with school as soon as possible. I paid the ultimate price due to my grades from my first bachelors being lackluster. To make matters worse, a degree in journalism isn’t exactly the most sought after skillset in Hawaii. Finally, after wandering from odd job to odd job, I decided to return to college with my family’s help.

This time around, rather than rushing to get another degree, I’m taking the time to actually learn something. I’m much more successful in my second attempt at college. My GPA is 3.50. It would be higher, but then the classes I took during my first attempt in the University of Hawaii system is bringing my GPA down.

Right now, I’m studying information technology at Kapiolani Community College. I was always interested in computers, math and business. In the past, I thought I wasn’t smart enough get past basic algebra, but then I learned to be successful at something that you have to have patience. In the future, I hope to work in data analytics or in web development.
